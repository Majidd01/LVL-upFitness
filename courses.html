Level 1 & 2 Diploma

Diploma in Fitness Training Level 1 & 2

Build the Foundation for a Successful Career in Fitness

The Diploma in Fitness Training Level 1 & 2 is an internationally aligned entry-level qualification designed for individuals who are passionate about health, fitness, and helping others achieve their goals. Whether you are looking to start a career as a Fitness Trainer, work within a gym environment, or develop your knowledge of exercise and wellness, this course provides the essential foundation required to enter the fitness industry with confidence.

Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical application, students will learn how the human body responds to exercise, how to assess clients safely, and how to design effective fitness programs for a variety of goals including fat loss, muscle gain, and overall health improvement.

Upon successful completion, graduates will possess the fundamental knowledge and practical skills required to begin working as a Fitness Trainer and progress towards advanced qualifications in Personal Training and specialized fitness disciplines.

Who Is This Course For?

This qualification is ideal for:

  • Aspiring Fitness Trainers
  • Gym Instructors and Health Club Staff
  • Fitness Enthusiasts Seeking Professional Knowledge
  • Individuals Looking to Start a Career in the Fitness Industry
  • Coaches Seeking Foundational Exercise Science Education
  • Anyone Interested in Health, Fitness, and Wellness

What You Will Learn

A comprehensive introduction to the key principles of fitness training and exercise instruction.

Fitness Assessments & Client Screening

Learn how to gather important client information through health, lifestyle, nutritional, and physical assessments to ensure safe and effective exercise participation.

Health-Related Fitness Testing

Understand how to conduct and interpret fitness tests to evaluate cardiovascular fitness, muscular strength, endurance, flexibility, and body composition.

Basic Human Anatomy & Physiology

Develop an understanding of the major body systems, muscles, bones, and joints involved in movement and exercise performance.

Principles of Exercise (FITT Principle)

Learn how Frequency, Intensity, Time, and Type influence exercise programming and client results.

Strength & Endurance Training Guidelines

Understand the fundamental principles behind resistance training and cardiovascular exercise for improving health, fitness, and performance.

Exercise Programming for Fat Loss & Muscle Gain

Learn how to design safe, structured, and goal-oriented exercise programs tailored to individual client needs.

Fundamentals of Nutrition

Gain an introduction to nutrition principles, energy balance, macronutrients, hydration, and healthy eating habits that support fitness goals.

Customer Service & Professional Conduct

Develop the communication and interpersonal skills required to build client trust, maintain professionalism, and deliver exceptional service within the fitness industry.

Practical Skills You Will Develop

  • Conduct basic client consultations and assessments
  • Perform health-related fitness testing
  • Explain exercise techniques safely and effectively
  • Design beginner fitness programs
  • Apply strength and endurance training principles
  • Support clients pursuing fat loss and muscle gain goals
  • Demonstrate professionalism within a gym or fitness environment
  • Communicate effectively with clients and colleagues

Course Delivery

  • Interactive Classroom Learning
  • Practical Workshops
  • Hands-On Exercise Instruction
  • Case Studies and Assessments
  • Industry-Relevant Training Materials
