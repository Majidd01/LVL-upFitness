2018 Founded

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Umar Farooq, Level Up Fitness was born from a vision to professionalize Pakistan's fitness industry. What began as a small training academy has grown into the country's leading fitness education institute.

We recognized a critical gap — passionate fitness enthusiasts lacked access to internationally accredited qualifications that could open doors to global careers. Level Up Fitness bridges that gap with REPs UK recognized programs, hands-on practical training, and dedicated career support.

Today, our graduates work across the UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and leading gyms throughout Pakistan.

Our Mission

To develop competent, ethical, and employable fitness professionals who elevate industry standards and improve lives through evidence-based training.

Our Vision

To become South Asia's leading fitness education provider — recognized globally for excellence in training, accreditation, and graduate outcomes.

Our Values

Integrity, practical excellence, student success, and continuous professional development guide everything we do.
